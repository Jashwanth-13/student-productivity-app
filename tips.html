Study Tips & Tricks

Plan in advance

Make a daily plan the night before — prioritize three most important tasks.

Pomodoro technique

Work for focused 25 mins then take a 5 min break. Repeat 4 times and take a longer break.

Active recall

Test yourself regularly instead of passive review — it sticks better.

Teach someone

If you can explain a concept simply, you understand it well.
